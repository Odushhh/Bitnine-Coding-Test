About Us


Ramani Digital - launched on the 12th of January 2020 - was an idea out of the blue to contribute to the Medical Tourism sector.

Organisations had to adjust & comply to the legislation at the time for all to stay home. All operations had to continue, and this was our birth.

All barriers were overcome through the help of a few trustees in our corner rooting for us, giving us time and stake to expand the company as well as provide services altogether.

We pride ourselves in producing results even while under pressure hence the saying, "Pressure makes diamonds."

Ramani Digital was initially started as a project to help struggling families in Samburu County, Kenya.

The Samburu tradition of using their customs and archaic methods of health treatment were becoming more outdated and actually causing more damage than relief.

Therefore, the startup was established to bring health amenities closer to home, since there were also hospitals, clinics many kilometres away from them.

Vision

To provide Worldwide Healthcare even to people living in remote areas.

Mission

Provision of adequate medical services to all citizens through Medical Tourism
