About Us


Ramani Digital - an aftermath of the pandemic that had everyone thinking out of the box in a bid to break away from their lifetime routines & do something different.

Most organisations at the time strongly emphasized on the idea of being physically present at work, till there was a law to be upheld by staying home. All operations had to continue, and this is where we come in.

Although the rise was accompanied by doubts and financial barriers, the few trustee we had in our corner rooting for us believed in us, giving us time and stake to expand the company as well as execute our main task at their companies.

We proud ourselves in using the most difficult of situations to produce the best - in relation to the saying "Pressure makes diamonds."
